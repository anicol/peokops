## Important Notes

### Phone Number Format
- Must include country code: **+1** for US/Canada
- Example: +16175551234
- Used for SMS delivery of micro-checks

### Store Codes
- Keep short (2-6 characters)
- Use letters/numbers only (no spaces or special characters)
- Examples: BG-DT, BG01, BGDOWN

### Timezones
Critical for accurate scheduling:
- Eastern: America/New_York
- Central: America/Chicago
- Mountain: America/Denver
- Pacific: America/Los_Angeles

### 7shifts Location IDs
Found in 7shifts:
1. Go to Settings → Locations
2. Click a location
3. Location ID is in the URL: `/locations/[ID]`
